Fried potatoes

Food
Ingredient Amount
Potatoes 600 g
Oil 50 ml
Onion 1 pcs
  1. Step 1: Clean, peel and slice potatoes and onions, pour oil into the pan and heat it
  2. Step 2: Fry potatoes and onions till they are golden brown
  3. Step 3: Add some salt and serve fried potatoes with maybe ketchup
